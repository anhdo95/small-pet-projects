Work out

To train the body by physical exercise
  1. Working out at a gym or swim twice a week is necessary for me
  2. He has many health issues so he choose to work out every day

Wear out

To make yourself/somebody feel very tired
  1. The past few days had really worn him out
  2. We extremely wore out because newcomers didn't integrate with our working tradition

Drop off

To fall into a light sleep
  1. I dropped off and missed the end of the movie

Can't stand + V-ing

To not be able to suffer/tolerant something
  1. I can't stand looking at drowsy people in my office

Can't help + V-ing

To not be able to control/stop something
  1. I can't help thinking about the prestigious/reputable achievement

Get round to

To find the time to do something
  1. It takes hours and hours just to get round to do this work
